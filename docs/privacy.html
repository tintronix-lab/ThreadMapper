Privacy Policy

ThreadMapper · Last updated 24 July 2026

ThreadMapper does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers. Everything the app records stays on your device.

What ThreadMapper reads

  • HomeKit accessory data — names, rooms, manufacturer, model, reachability and battery level of accessories in your home. This is read from Apple's HomeKit framework with your permission and is used to build your Thread mesh view.
  • Approximate location — only while you are actively running a survey walk, and only to tag signal readings so they can be shown on a heatmap. Location is requested when a survey begins, never at launch. Readings without a location fix are stored without coordinates rather than being given fabricated ones.
  • Signal measurements — response-quality estimates the app derives itself by timing accessory responses.

Where it is stored

All data — device history, survey points, activity events, notes and settings — is stored locally on your device (in the app's own container, an App Group shared with the widget and Apple Watch app, and standard user preferences). None of it is uploaded to us. We operate no servers and receive no copy of it.

Artificial intelligence features

On iOS 26 and later, ThreadMapper's AI features (Insights, Network Assistant, troubleshooting and reports) run entirely on device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. Your network data is not sent to us or to any third-party AI service.

Network connections

ThreadMapper makes no analytics or advertising requests. It connects to the internet only if you explicitly configure the optional Thread Border Router integration, in which case it talks directly to the address you enter on your own local network.

Purchases

ThreadMapper Pro is handled entirely by Apple's In-App Purchase system. We never see or store your payment details. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your data to anyone, because we never receive it. Anything you choose to export (a CSV of survey data or a diagnostic PDF) leaves the app only when you deliberately share it, and goes wherever you send it.

Children

ThreadMapper is a network utility and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Your control

You can revoke HomeKit or Location permission at any time in iOS Settings. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. The app also provides in-app controls to clear activity history and saved survey data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
